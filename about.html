Our story

A coastal gift shop on the boardwalk

Bayshore Gifts & Gallery keeps the bright side of the coast in one sunny shop, from local art to the souvenir you did not know you needed.

A family shop on the bay

Wrapped in a mural, run by a family

We are a family run gift shop and gallery at the south end of the Coos Bay Boardwalk, wrapped in a hand painted octopus and kelp mural. Inside you will find coastal apparel, handmade jewelry, local art, toys and kites, and a whole wall of souvenirs and sundries.

Most of what we carry is sustainably sourced from small businesses and Oregon makers, with minimal plastics and plenty of made in the USA. The artist series socks are part of an Art Giveback program, so a piece of every pair goes back to working artists and community mural projects.

Vintage 101 Marketplace is right next door if you are in the mood for antiques and vintage finds. Come wander both, take your time, and leave with a little of the coast in your bag.

What we stand for

Good gifts, made and given thoughtfully

  • Made in the USA and woman owned
  • Minimal plastics, sustainably sourced
  • Local Oregon artists and small makers
  • The Art Giveback supports working artists and murals
  • Everyone welcome
