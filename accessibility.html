Accessibility

We want every visitor to be able to use this site and enjoy the shop.

Bayshore Gifts & Gallery is committed to keeping this website usable for everyone, and we aim to meet WCAG 2.2 Level AA.

What we have done

  • Keyboard accessible navigation and a skip to content link.
  • Text and background colors chosen for readable contrast.
  • Descriptive alt text on images.
  • A visible focus outline so you can see where you are.
  • A pause control on the scrolling banner.
  • Respect for the reduce motion setting in your browser or device.
  • The site works without JavaScript.

In the shop

The shop is on the Coos Bay Boardwalk, with easy parking nearby. If you have any access questions before you stop in, please call us at (541) 808-0878 and we will be glad to help.

Tell us

If you find something hard to use, email bayshoregiftscoosbay@gmail.com or call (541) 808-0878 and we will do our best to fix it.
