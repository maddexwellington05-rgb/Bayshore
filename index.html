Buy local, give back

A shop wrapped in a mural, and giving back to more

The artist series socks on the rack are part of an Art Giveback program, so a piece of every pair supports working artists and community mural projects. Stained glass, prints, and cards from coastal makers fill the shelves around them.

  • Art that supports artists and murals
  • Made by small businesses and Oregon makers
  • Minimal plastics, sustainably sourced
